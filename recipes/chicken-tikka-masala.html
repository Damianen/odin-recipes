Chicken tikka masala

Description:

Chicken thighs coated in tikka masala sauce with rice and broccoli. Using a store bought tikka masala sauce can speed up the time it takes to cook this meal.
Using pre-made sauces for ease isn't something I do often but it greatly speeds up the process.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs (908 g) boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 16 oz (454 g) frozen broccoli
  • 6 oz (170 g) frozen peas
  • 8 oz (227 g) shredded carrots
  • 1.25 cups (313 g) tikka masala sauce
  • 2.5 cups (375 g) cooked rice
  • 1 tbsp (15 ml) olive oil
  • Parsley

Instructions:

  1. Cook enough rice to have 2 ½ cups worth.
  2. Heat ½ tbsp of oil in a cast iron grill or skillet over medium heat. Cook the chicken. Cut into 1 inch pieces.
  3. In a skillet, heat ½ tbsp of oil over medium heat and add the peas and carrots. Cook for a few minutes.
  4. Add the chicken and sauce. Stir to incorporate.
  5. Cook the broccoli in the microwave.

Plating:

  1. This recipe makes 5 servings. Divide your ingredients evenly between 5 containers. Put ½ cup of rice in each dish.
